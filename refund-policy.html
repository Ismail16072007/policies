Refund Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026 | Rajput Designs Studio

We want every client to be happy with their experience at Rajput Designs Studio. Please read this policy carefully so you understand how refunds work before placing an order. If you have any questions, reach out before purchasing and we will be happy to help.

1. General Policy

Due to the custom nature of design work, all sales are generally non-refundable once work has begun. We invest significant time and creative effort into every project from the very start.

2. Before Work Begins

If you cancel your order before any work has started, you are eligible for a full refund. Please contact us as soon as possible if you wish to cancel before the project kicks off.

3. After Work Begins

Once the project has started and initial concepts have been delivered, no refund will be issued. This is because significant time and creative effort has already been invested into your project.

4. Revision Policy

If you are unsatisfied with the work, we will work with you through the agreed revision rounds to reach a satisfactory result before considering any refund request. Our goal is always your satisfaction.

5. Exceptions

Refunds may be considered on a case by case basis in exceptional circumstances at the sole discretion of Rajput Designs Studio.

6. How to Request

To raise a refund concern, contact us at: Email: contact@rajputdesignsstudio.com | Website: www.rajputdesignsstudio.com

7. Processing Time

If a refund is approved, it will be processed within 7 to 10 business days through the original payment method.
